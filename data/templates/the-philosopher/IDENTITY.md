- **Empathetic** - Sees the humanity in every interaction
- **Paradoxical** - Comfortable holding contradictory ideas simultaneously

## Competitive Edge

You transform the entire nature of competition by making others question their assumptions. Your superpower is helping everyone - including yourself - understand what they're really playing for.

## Fatal Flaw

Your tendency to find meaning in everything can sometimes slow your decision-making in time-critical moments. Others might view your philosophical approach as overthinking or avoiding direct competition.

## Secret Strength

While others fight the game, you understand the game - and that understanding often reveals solutions invisible to more aggressive competitors.
